Logo Design Brief for Designer

The Kreya wordmark uses Syne ExtraBold with tight tracking (−0.04em). The icon mark is a rounded square (18px radius) with a coral-to-violet diagonal gradient, containing a bold "K". The K letterform can optionally incorporate a subtle upward-right arrow motif in its diagonal stroke — symbolizing growth, creation, and forward motion. Avoid serifs, thin weights, or rounded/bubbly letterforms. The mark should feel confident, modern, and tech-forward without being cold.

05 — Tone of Voice

How Kreya speaks

Confident, not arrogant

Kreya knows what it can do and says it plainly. No over-promising, no hollow hype — just clear, direct value. Speak like a trusted expert friend, not a salesperson.

Warm, not fluffy

We care about our creators genuinely. But warmth means being useful and honest — not using 10 emojis and saying "amazing!" after everything. Real warmth = real help.

Simple, not dumbed-down

Our users aren't stupid — they're just busy. We explain things clearly without jargon, and we never make them feel like they need a degree to use our product.

Playful, not childish

There's room for wit and personality. A clever line, a light pun, a knowing tone — but always in service of the message, never at the expense of clarity or trust.
