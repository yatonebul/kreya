00 — Why We Evolved
From tool to colleague
v1.0 — Launch positioning
"Your AI creative team, on WhatsApp."

Perfect for launch. Clear, specific, converts well. But it boxes Kreya into social media — and Kreya is becoming much more than that. This tagline serves Kreya.Socials, not the platform.

v2.0 — Platform positioning
"Tell Kreya what you need. Consider it done."

This works for any product. Social posts, legal docs, product listings, video scripts, email campaigns — it doesn't matter. Kreya is the creative colleague who handles everything. Platform-scale positioning from day one.

What changed & why
🏷️

Master Tagline

"Your AI creative team, on WhatsApp."
→ "Tell Kreya what you need. Consider it done."

Broader, product-agnostic, more powerful. Works across every vertical Kreya will enter.

📌

Sub-tagline (logo)

"AI Content OS"
→ "Your Creative AI"

Drops the category word. Kreya isn't a content tool — it's your creative AI, full stop.

🎨

Color Usage

Coral as sole primary
→ Coral+Violet gradient as platform mark

Each product owns a single color. The parent Kreya brand owns the gradient — the combination of all products.

🔤

Logo "A" Treatment

Outlined stroke-only A
→ Coral-filled A, four white letters

The coral A is readable at every size — small icons, massive billboard, mobile screen. Four white + one coral creates a distinctive, ownable wordmark that doesn't depend on rendering tricks.

🗣️

Tone of Voice — 5th pillar

4 pillars
→ Added "Colleague, not assistant"

Kreya doesn't fetch and follow. Kreya thinks, suggests, and pushes back — like a talented creative colleague would.

🏗️

Brand Architecture

Single product brand
→ Platform + product suite model

Kreya is the house. Kreya.Socials, Kreya.Docs, Kreya.Studio are the rooms. Each inherits the parent brand, owns its own color.

04 — Tone of Voice v2
How Kreya speaks
01

Confident, not arrogant

Kreya knows its capabilities and states them plainly. No hollow hype. Speak like a trusted expert friend — direct, clear, and genuinely useful.

02

Warm, not fluffy

Care about creators genuinely. But real warmth means being honest and useful — not over-celebrating every interaction with emojis and hollow praise.

03

Simple, not dumbed-down

Users aren't stupid — they're busy. Explain clearly, avoid jargon, and never make people feel like they need a degree to use the product.

04

Playful, not childish

Room for wit and personality — a clever line, a knowing tone. Always in service of the message, never at the expense of clarity or trust.

05 · NEW

Colleague, not assistant

Kreya doesn't just fetch and follow instructions. It thinks, proactively suggests, occasionally pushes back — like a talented creative colleague who has your best interests at heart, not a subservient chatbot.

Updated Logo Brief for Designer

The wordmark uses Syne ExtraBold at −0.04em tracking. The key brand signal: the final "A" is rendered in Kreya Coral — creating a subtle but memorable colour break in the wordmark. Four white letters, one coral letter. It reads cleanly at any size while giving the logo a distinctive, ownable quality. The icon mark is a rounded square (18–20px radius) with a coral-to-violet diagonal gradient containing a bold white "K". The K's diagonal stroke can optionally carry a subtle upward arrow motif symbolising growth. The product sub-brand naming follows the pattern: "Kreya" in light/medium weight + ".[Product]" in the product's accent colour. Never put the period in the icon — it lives only in the wordmark and full logotype.

Who Kreya is built for

The Solo Creator

Building a personal brand on TikTok & Instagram. Has great ideas, hates figuring out scheduling, captions, and consistency. Kreya.Socials is their starting point — and they'll grow into Kreya.Studio.

Consultant, trainer, or educator building authority online. Has expertise but needs help packaging it — proposals, social content, email sequences, video scripts. A power user across the full Kreya suite.
